ABOUT ME

I fell in love with Video Games when my parents gave me my first Game Boy. Since then, I have become fascinated with the art behind every single game that I've played. So here I am, pursuing my passion for Game Art and deepening my understanding of the Art. Photography is my second great love. I picked up my first camera and started taking photos under the gentle guidance and teaching of my boyfriend/photographer. Now I use photography as a way for me to journal and immortalise precious moments.
